PIR Insulation

PIR (polyisocyanurate) insulation is produced as a board by combining various chemicals. PIR insulation has very good thermal value and for this reason is used in many different areas such as external walls in houses and even under the foundations in new build houses. The board also comes with a foil face which acts as a vapour control layer.
