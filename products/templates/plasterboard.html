Plasterboard

Plasterboard is made up of dry gypsum that is sandwiched between a facer and a backer. It is used to make interior walls and ceilings. By adding certain additives we are able to make the board more suited to certain environments such as shower rooms, noisy areas or places where there is a need for higher fire protection.
