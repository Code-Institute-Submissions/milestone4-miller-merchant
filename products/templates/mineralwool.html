Mineral Wool Insulation

Mineral insulation can be separated into two different types either glass or rock. The glass/rock is put through a kiln at a very high temperature to create the finished product. Due to the density and the air gaps within the rolls mineral wool makes for a good sound insulator either between internal walls or below separating floors. The number at the end of the title name refers to the thermal value of the product with the lower being a having a better thermal value.
