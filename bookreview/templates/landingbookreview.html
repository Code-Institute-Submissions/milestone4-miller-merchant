Book Of The Month

Each month we have a competition where you are able to vote and win. On the first of each month we preview three books on Miller Merchant and allow you to vote on what one you think we should add to our store.

Once the votes have been calculated and the winning book confirmed at the end of the month it will then be added. What makes it special is that the first 100 copies of the winning book will be signed by the author so make sure that you are quick to grab your copy.

Everyone that votes is automatically enrolled into our prize draw and the winner will be selected at random and the lucky voter will win a copy of the signed book with a personalised message from the author.

Every month we will have an exclusive interview with the author of the winning book. They will give you an insight into the thinking behind the book and really set the scene while your copy is on its way to you.

To get access to the competition and read the authors interview you must be a member of our site. If you are already a member then select what area you want to look at.
